About Me

Hello! I’m Arushi Sareen, currently pursuing an M.A. in Economics at Ashoka University. With a strong background in economics and a keen interest in research, data analysis, and market trends, I’m passionate about exploring the intersection of finance, business, and policy. I hold an undergraduate degree in economics from University of Delhi.
As a founding member of Eco Spectrum, my experience in academic publishing deepened my analytical and writing skills. Additionally, I served as the Skill Enhancement Head under 'Stitching the Change', an Enactus India project focused on vocational training for underprivileged communities. Whether through data-driven insights, policy research, or strategic consulting, I aim to contribute to impactful solutions.
My interdisciplinary background equips me with both qualitative and quantitative approaches to problem-solving. I am actively seeking opportunities in analytics, research, or consulting that align with my expertise and aspirations. Always open to discussions and collaborations!
